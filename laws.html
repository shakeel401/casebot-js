18 U.S. Code § 2 | Principals

This section states that anyone who commits an offense against the United States or aids, abets, counsels, commands, induces, or procures its commission is punishable as a principal.

18 U.S. Code § 3 | Accessory after the fact

This section addresses those who assist an offender after the commission of a crime, with the intent to hinder or prevent their arrest, trial, or punishment.

18 U.S. Code § 4 | Misprision of felony

This section criminalizes the act of concealing and not reporting a felony to the authorities.

18 U.S. Code § 32 | Destruction of aircraft or aircraft facilities

This section penalizes the destruction, damage, or disabling of any aircraft, aircraft engine, propeller, or other property or facility used in air commerce.

18 U.S. Code § 33 | Destruction of motor vehicles or motor vehicle facilities

This section penalizes the destruction or disabling of motor vehicles or motor vehicle facilities, with the intent to endanger safety or with reckless disregard for safety.

18 U.S. Code § 34 | Penalty when death results

This section increases the penalty for violations of certain sections when the conduct results in the death of any person.

18 U.S. Code § 35 | Imparting or conveying false information

This section penalizes anyone who imparts or conveys false information knowing it to be false, regarding violations related to aircraft and motor vehicle facilities.

18 U.S. Code § 111 | Assaulting, resisting, or impeding certain officers or employees

This section penalizes assaults, resistance, or impeding of certain officers or employees of the United States while they are engaged in their official duties.

18 U.S. Code § 112 | Protection of foreign officials, official guests, and internationally protected persons

This section provides for the protection of foreign officials, official guests, and internationally protected persons from assault, intimidation, or violence.

This section defines and penalizes various forms of assault that occur within the special maritime and territorial jurisdiction of the United States.

18 U.S. Code § 115 | Influencing, impeding, or retaliating against a Federal official by threatening or injuring a family member
